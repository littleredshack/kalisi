Time sync issues?

Ensure your device clock is synchronized. On most devices:

  • iPhone: Settings → General → Date & Time → Enable "Set Automatically"
  • Android: Settings → System → Date & Time → Enable "Automatic date & time"
  • Desktop: Sync with internet time server

Want to start fresh?

You can reset your MFA setup:

  1. Delete the EDT account from your authenticator app
  2. Click "Reset via Email" to receive a reset link
  3. Follow the email instructions to set up MFA again
warning This will temporarily disable 2FA on your account until you set it up again.
